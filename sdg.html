NEL-BOT

Revolutionizing Ocean Cleanup with Autonomous Technology

Ocean pollution, particularly plastic waste, is one of the most pressing environmental challenges of our time. Our team’s project, NELBot, was designed to address this issue through innovation, sustainability, and advanced engineering. NELBot is an autonomous submarine robot that collects underwater debris, prioritizing efficiency and environmental conservation. By leveraging artificial intelligence, sustainable materials, and precision mechanical systems, NELBot aims to make a significant impact in the fight against marine pollution.

At the core of NELBot's capabilities is its advanced sensing and AI-driven perception system. Using the YOLOv5s machine learning model, NELBot can accurately detect and differentiate between ocean debris and marine life. This ensures that NELBot focuses its efforts on collecting trash, minimizing harm to the delicate marine ecosystem. The robot uses a hybrid collection technique, deploying robotic arms for debris adhered to the ocean floor while utilizing interconnected baskets for floating debris clusters. This design not only reduces energy consumption but also extends the robot’s operational duration.

Mechanically, NELBot is built to withstand the harsh underwater environment. Its main casing is constructed from carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP), which offers excellent strength and corrosion resistance. Additionally, recycled steel and aluminum alloys are used for structural components, aligning with our commitment to sustainable materials. Biocomposites and bio- based polymers are used in non-structural parts, further reducing the vehicle’s environmental impact. NELBot’s propulsion and power systems are designed for efficiency and endurance. Powered by solar panels and 24V batteries, NELBot operates autonomously for extended periods, ensuring maximum impact with minimal energy use. This hybrid power approach makes NELBot a sustainable solution to marine pollution.

NEL Nights event

NEL Nights" Webinar Series - Unveiling the Future of Engineering with AI!

The "NEL Nights" webinar series is a powerful platform aimed at building the capacities of young engineers by equipping them with essential knowledge of artificial intelligence (AI) and its practical applications in their fields. By exploring AI's role in geographic systems, building technologies, mechanical engineering, and robotics, this event helps future engineers understandhow to leverage AI to innovate within their work. More than just technical knowledge, the series also contributes to global efforts toward achieving the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) by promoting sustainable engineering solutions. By integrating AI, young engineers can drive advancements that address challenges in infrastructure, industry, and environmental sustainability.
